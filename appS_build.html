Appendix S Supplementary Figures For Luminous Egress Path Markings

SECTION BC S101
GENERAL

Legislative History:
Local Law 141/13 (2014 Code Revision Cycle)
S101.1 Scope.

The figures of this appendix shall supplement the provisions of luminous egress path markings in Section 1024 and are intended for illustrative purposes. Where there is a conflict between the figures and the provisions in Section 1024, the provisions in Section 1024 shall govern.
